DHOKLA

Dhokla is a savoury sponge dish that is native to the Indian state of Gujarat and parts of adjacent states, and is popular throughout the country. It is made with a fermented batter that is steamed to a cake-like consistency.

CHHOLE KULCHE

Chhole Kulche is a popular North Indian street food consisting of spiced chickpeas (chhole) served with leavened bread (kulche). The chickpeas are cooked in a blend of cumin, coriander, and garam masala, creating a rich curry.

GULAB JAMUN

Gulab Jamun is a delightful Indian dessert made of deep-fried milk-based dumplings soaked in sugar syrup. These golden-brown dumplings, flavored with cardamom or rose water, offer a sweet and indulgent treat.

IDLI VADA

Idli Vada is a South Indian dish featuring steamed rice cakes (idli) and fried lentil donuts (vada). This popular breakfast combo is often served with chutney and sambar for a delicious meal.

Vada Pav is a popular Indian street food consisting of a spicy potato dumpling (vada) sandwiched in a pav (soft bread roll). Often accompanied by chutneys, it's a flavorful and iconic snack.
